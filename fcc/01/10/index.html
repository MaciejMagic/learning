Nicola Tesla

One of the greatest inventors of our time

Important events in Nicola Tesla's life

  • 1856 - born in the village of Smljan in the Austrian Empire (modern day Croatia)
  • 1875 - enrolled at the Imperial-Royal Technical College in Graz
  • 1877 - fascination with electricity began. Presented by Professor Jakob Pöschl and described how he made suggestions on improving the design of an electric motor the professor was demonstrating
  • 1882 - worked in Paris with Continental Edison Company on installing indoor incandescent lighting citywide in large scale
  • 1891 - US citizenship and Tesla Coil patent
  • 1890s through 1906 - work on numerous wireless power transfer technology devices
  • 1943 - died at the age of 86 in a hotel room

“The scientists of today think deeply instead of clearly. One must be sane to think clearly, but one can think deeply and be quite insane.” ― Nikola Tesla
